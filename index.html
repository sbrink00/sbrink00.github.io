Delicious Grilled Cheese

    Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

    Directions

  1. Put a pan on the stove, butter the pan, and set the stove to a low or medium heat
  2. Butter one side of both pieces of bread
  3. Put the cheese in between the 2 slices,
    and place the sandwich in the pan
  4. Grill until the bottom side is brown
  5. Flip the sandwich and grill until the other side is brown
  6. Enjoy your delicious sandwich
